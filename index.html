Who this is for

This comparison is built mainly for local travellers, expats, and anyone who wants a straightforward view of what’s available in each country — not just tourist SIMs. The table mixes local prepaid, postpaid, and common no-contract options because those are the plans residents actually use.

How we gather this data

We pull prices and plan details from public comparison sources, telco sites, and traveller/local guides. Plans are checked against recent sources every week and marked with eSIM confidence levels so you can see at a glance what’s reliable, likely supported, or unconfirmed.
