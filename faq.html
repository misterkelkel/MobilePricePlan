Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about plans, eSIM, and coverage

Which plan has the most data?

Use the table on the main page — the plan with the highest GB value usually offers the most data. This varies by country and operator, so compare directly before committing.

Do any plans include roaming?

Yes. Many plans include roaming data, regional bundles, or added roaming passes. Look for the roaming notes in the table and confirm current terms with the operator before signing up.

What is eSIM?

eSIM lets you activate your plan digitally — no physical SIM needed. Look for Yes or Likely in the eSIM column of the plan table.

Are the prices up to date?

Plans and prices change regularly. We update the tables on a weekly cadence. For the latest price, confirm directly with each telco.

How do I know if a plan is right for me?

Think about your main use: daily local use, short-term travel, cross-border work, or roaming-heavy trips. If you’re still unsure, use the contact form on the About page and describe your use case.
